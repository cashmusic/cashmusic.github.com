CASH Music

Nonprofit + FLOSS + Music tech

CASH Music is a nonprofit organization that builds open source digital tools for musicians and labels. Our mission is to help educate and empower artists and their fans to foster a more viable and sustainable future for music.

We’re building a free and open platform that gives musicians and labels a no-programming environment for music tech online. You download it, the platform works with your website, integrates with what you’ve got, and plays nice with services like SoundCloud, PayPal, Facebook, Twitter, MailChimp, and more.
